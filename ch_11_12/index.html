PIAIC

PRESIDENTIAL INITIATIVE FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND COMPUTING

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing (PIAIC) is a privately funded not-for-profit educational initiative started by President of Pakistan, Dr. Arif Alvi, Kazi Rahat Ali, and Zia Khan to help promote education, research, and business opportunities in emerging tech fields like:

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing

The Cloud Native Computing program prepares the students to program in open source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and React Native, while introducing the students to development of Microservices and software development techniques on cloud platforms like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence program takes the student through the foundation of AI technology, teaching programming in open source languages like Python, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow 2.0, and Flask, and deploying practical AI solutions with real-life examples of modern AI in practice.

Blockchain

The Blockchain program emphasizes understanding blockchain technologies like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric) and programming in languages such as Solidity, JavaScript on frameworks like Node.js. The program also covers Distributed Apps (DApps), Web3, Truffle, and Blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS).

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things program introduces students to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes, with a focus on developing Microservices for IoT Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
